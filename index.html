We areCitizents / People

Art Project United is a visual art exhibition using social media challenging people and photographers both professional and amateur to set up, join together and their powerful images of people wearing the united tank top to show awareness concern us expressing our stories strength pride beauty history and that we are united.

Our Mission Black / Brown

We are concerned and stan together over the alarming and disturbing rate of murders of black and browns skin men, woman and children in this country.

Our goal is showing this country and the world our diversity history pride and unity! That's we stand aware strong united worthy of a life and worth fighting for!

Global Movement Unity / Pride

We are not only making history we are creating our own new history at the same time.

I don't think this has ever been done before on this scale ever. We will have images being posted from all over the country maybe the world.

Be Part of ItShare / Connect

All we ask is that your subjects are photographed in the united tank tops and we can see the united logo so everyone will know that your images are supporting and part of the art project united exhibition.
